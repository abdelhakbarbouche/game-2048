2048
guide
The goal of the game is to combine numbers to create bigger number tiles in order to get to the magic number, 2048. The rules of how to play 2048 are simple. You have four ways to move the tiles: left, right, up, and down. You combine numbers like 2 and 2 to make a 4 tile, 4 and 4 to make an 8 tile, and so on and so forth. A low number such as a 2 or 4 will appear in a random spot with every move you make.
